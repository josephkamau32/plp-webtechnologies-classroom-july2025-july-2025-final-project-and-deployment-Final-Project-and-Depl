Our Story

Discover the passion behind our artisanal coffee blends

Our Journey

Founded in 2017, Artisanal Coffee Blends began as a small passion project between two coffee enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves to experience truly exceptional coffee. What started as a humble roastery in Portland has grown into a specialty coffee company known for our commitment to quality and sustainability.

We travel the world to source the finest beans, building direct relationships with farmers who share our values of ethical growing practices and environmental stewardship. Each batch is carefully roasted in small quantities to bring out the unique characteristics of every bean.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great coffee is an experience that engages all the senses. From the aroma that greets you in the morning to the last sip of your cup, we strive to create moments of joy and connection through our carefully crafted blends.

Sustainability

We partner with farms that practice organic farming and fair labor practices.

Quality

Every batch is roasted to perfection and tested for consistency and flavor.

Community

We support local coffee culture and education through workshops and events.
