Brewing Guide

Master the art of coffee brewing at home

Brewing Methods

Pour Over Brewing

The pour over method allows you to control every aspect of the brewing process, resulting in a clean, flavorful cup that highlights the unique characteristics of the coffee.

What You'll Need:

  • Pour over brewer (V60, Chemex, or Kalita Wave)
  • Paper filters
  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Scale
  • Timer
  • Freshly ground coffee

Steps:

  1. Boil water to 200째F (93째C)
  2. Place filter in brewer and rinse with hot water
  3. Add 22g of medium-fine ground coffee
  4. Pour 50g of water to bloom, wait 30 seconds
  5. Slowly pour remaining water in circular motions (total water: 360g)
  6. Total brew time should be 2:30-3:00 minutes
  7. Remove filter and enjoy

French Press Brewing

The French press method produces a rich, full-bodied cup with more oils and sediments than other methods, creating a robust flavor profile.

What You'll Need:

  • French press
  • Coarse ground coffee
  • Hot water
  • Timer
  • Scale (optional but recommended)

Steps:

  1. Boil water to 200째F (93째C)
  2. Add 30g of coarse ground coffee to the French press
  3. Pour 500g of water, ensuring all grounds are saturated
  4. Place lid on top with plunger pulled up
  5. Steep for 4 minutes
  6. Slowly press plunger down
  7. Pour immediately to prevent over-extraction
